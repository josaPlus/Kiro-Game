# Factory Safety Inspector

A top-down factory safety simulation game built with Python and Pygame.

The player acts as a Safety & Hygiene Coordinator, inspecting factories for hazards, managing workers, and enforcing PPE compliance. Three factory environments are planned: Chemical Plant (hard), Auto Assembly (medium), and Warehouse (easy).

The game is currently in early alpha (v0.1). Core navigation and scene scaffolding are in place; actual gameplay mechanics are not yet implemented.
